## Context

What problem are we solving? What forces are at play (technical,
organisational, regulatory)? Why is this decision needed *now*?

## Decision

The one-paragraph "we will do X". Imperative present tense. Avoid
hedging language — that goes in Consequences.

## Consequences

### Positive

- What this unlocks or simplifies.

### Negative

- What this costs or constrains.

### Neutral

- Anything noteworthy that's neither a win nor a loss but a future
  reader needs to know.

## Alternatives considered

Short bullet per alternative + the reason it was rejected. Including
the alternatives is the point of the ADR — otherwise the decision
looks like the only possible one.
